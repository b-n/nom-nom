Dutch Lolly Log

last edited 10 months ago

A twist on a New Zealand classic with ingredients found in The Netherlands

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients except for the coconut in a bowl

  2. Dust a bench top with the coconut. Split the mixture into quarters, and roll the mixture into logs. Ensure the outside is coated in the coconut so it isn't sticky

  3. Place the logs onto aluminium foil covered plates and put into the fridge

  4. Let the mixture cool for at least 2 hours, but preferably over night

Tips:

  1. See the Dutch version for the names of the ingredients that you can find in the supermarket

  2. It is best to omit a small amount of melted butter before mixing. Enough butter should be used to bind, but it shouldn't be too wet

  3. It's nice to have a small amount of crunch sometimes, so don't crush the biscuits too much

Ingredients

  • 390 grams Schuimpjes - Broken into small pieces

  • 2 packets (500 grams) Lotus Speculoos biscuits - Crushed/crumbed

  • 1 tin (400 gram) Sweetened Condensed Milk

  • 225 grams salted butter - melted

  • Grated dried coconut
